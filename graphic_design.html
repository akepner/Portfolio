Peanut Butter How-To Project

The Ultimate Peanut Butter Milkshake was created by a brother and sister seeking to find a more delicious solution to their father's nightly, boring vanilla milkshake. This recipe was created, then introduced to the children's father in hopes tat he would not be offended. Fortunately, He loved it, and the Ultimate Peanut Butter Milkshake was born! This How-To Guide expresses the steps and process used to create the delectable milkshake!

"Snackle Magazine"

"Snackle Magazine" is a magazine dedicated to providing healthy recipes for snacking. The cover was influenced by the beautiful colors of summer and the articles throughout are prevalent to summer in general!

"Tall Girl, Small Feet" Book Series
