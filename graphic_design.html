Peanut Butter How-To Project

The Ultimate Peanut Butter Milkshake was created by a brother and sister seeking to find a more delicious solution to their father's nightly, boring vanilla milkshake. This recipe was created, then introduced to the children's father in hopes that he would not be offended. Fortunately, he loved it, and the Ultimate Peanut Butter Milkshake was born! This How-To Guide expresses the steps and process used to create the delectable milkshake!
